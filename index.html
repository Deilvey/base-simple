Is this free to use?

Yes. Base Portfolio Tracker is completely free. No hidden fees, no subscriptions. We make money through optional premium features.

Do you store my private keys?

Never. We're non-custodial. Your wallet connects directly to Base chain. We can't access your funds.

Which wallets are supported?

MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, WalletConnect, Rainbow, and any wallet that supports Base chain.

Can I track multiple wallets?

Yes. Add unlimited wallets and switch between them instantly. Perfect for managing multiple portfolios.
